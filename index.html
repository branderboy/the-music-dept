03 · Proprietary Software

Music Dept Studio Tools™

A major component of this project is developing custom WordPress plugins that turn your website into an interactive platform. These tools generate qualified leads, build massive topical authority, and create software assets your competitors cannot easily replicate.

🛠️
The Hook

Interactive Lead Generators

Strategy: Provide immediate utility in exchange for an email address and project data. You stop hoping they fill out a contact form, and start giving them a reason to.

The Software Suite

  1. The Missing Royalty Finder Signature ToolOne of the strongest opportunities for lead gen. Artists answer a few quick questions (Are you registered with ASCAP/BMI? SoundExchange? The MLC? Songtrust? YouTube Content ID?). The tool identifies potential missing registrations and revenue streams, capturing their email to send the customized report.
  2. Music Release PlannerUser inputs release date, budget, and genre. The tool outputs a customized 90-day release strategy calendar.
  3. Recording Budget CalculatorArtists input track counts and scope to estimate studio time and mixing costs.
  4. EP Timeline GeneratorCreates an eight-week production schedule tailored to their project.
  5. Band Practice PlannerGenerates structured, time-boxed rehearsal schedules to keep bands efficient.

Additional tools planned: Music Marketing Checklist, Studio Session Checklist, Podcast Launch Planner, Album Budget Planner, Tour Rehearsal Planner, Equipment Checklist Generator, and Music Career Roadmap.
